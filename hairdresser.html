Booking system for hairstylists

Project goals: Build a booking system for customers, simplify the booking process and reduce the cost of external third party suppliers. By updating their digital presence, the client wanted increased prominence and to set their business apart from competition in a highly competitive market thus increasing their customer base. Role:Conduct user research and competitive analysis, translate diverse feedback inputs into actionable insights in the rebranding process, wireframe and design. Design challenge highlights: Conversion. Remove third party and eliminate redundant steps of the booking process. How did I tackle it? User research showed that users don't like to give too much information to book a session and they don't want to repeat the process every time. Designed a simple user flow of calendar, reduced the booking process so that the user can book desired service under a minute and decreased the response time even further with the next login. Impact Increase ROI, increase customer base, new employees.
